Nutrition Month 2026

What is Nutrition Month?

Nutrition Month is celebrated every July in the Philippines. It reminds everyone to eat healthy foods, exercise regularly, and practice a healthy lifestyle.

Healthy Foods

Fruits, vegetables, fish, eggs, rice, and milk are examples of healthy foods that help us grow strong and stay healthy.

Tips for a Healthy Life

Eat more fruits and vegetables.

Drink plenty of water every day.

Exercise or play sports regularly.

Get enough sleep every night.

Avoid eating too much junk food.

Message

Good nutrition helps us stay active, learn better in school, and protect our bodies from sickness.

"Healthy Food, Healthy Body, Healthy Future!"
